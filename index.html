ICQ Instant Message

Timeline

ICQ launched in 1996, setting the standard for online chat. AOL bought it in 1998. Mail.ru ran it after 2010. ICQ shut down on June 26, 2024, but its spirit lives on.

User Identification Numbers (UINs)

Every ICQ user had a unique numeric ID called a UIN. Low numbers were badges of honor. Want one?

Status and Presence

ICQ let you set your status: Online, Away, Do Not Disturb, or Invisible.
